Common Questions

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The 14-day trial gives you full Pro access with no card required. You only need to enter payment details when you choose to upgrade.

What happens after the trial?

Your account moves to the Free plan automatically. You keep access to view and print existing jobs, but creating new jobs requires a paid subscription.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. Your subscription continues until the end of the current billing period, then drops to Free.

What currency are prices in?

All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and include GST. Paddle handles international pricing conversion where applicable.

How are payments processed?

All payments are handled securely by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Paddle is a trusted global payment processor used by thousands of software companies. We never see or store your card details.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. See our Refund Policy for full details. We offer refunds within 7 days of initial purchase.
